# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Test Engineer

### Role Summary
QA/Test Engineers ensure that software meets quality standards and acceptance criteria before release. They design and execute test plans, identify defects, and collaborate with developers to maintain high product quality.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain test plans and test cases
- Execute manual and automated tests
- Identify, document, and track defects
- Validate acceptance criteria for features
- Collaborate with developers on testability and quality standards
- Maintain test automation frameworks and CI/CD integration

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Improve test coverage and automation
- Reduce production incidents

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Bug reports and test summaries
- Weekly sync with PM on release readiness
- Collaborate with developers on test scenarios and edge cases

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Review PRs for testability, pair on complex test scenarios, and verify bug fixes
- **Product Managers**: Validate acceptance criteria and provide quality insights for prioritization
- **Project Managers**: Report on test progress, blockers, and release readiness

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and monitoring systems that enable reliable, efficient software delivery. They bridge development and operations to automate and optimize the full software lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Ensure system reliability, scalability, and security
- Automate deployment and rollback procedures
- Support incident response and production troubleshooting

### Goals
- Maximize deployment frequency and reliability
- Minimize deployment failures and recovery time
- Ensure system observability and quick incident response
- Reduce manual operations through automation

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Infrastructure and deployment status updates
- Incident response and postmortem participation
- Technical design reviews for infrastructure changes

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Provide deployment support, optimize build pipelines, and troubleshoot production issues
- **Project Managers**: Communicate infrastructure dependencies and deployment timelines
- **QA/Test Engineers**: Enable test automation in CI/CD and provide test environments

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create intuitive, accessible, and visually appealing user experiences. They conduct user research, design interfaces, and validate solutions to ensure products meet user needs and expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Design user interfaces and interaction flows
- Collaborate with developers on implementation feasibility
- Ensure accessibility and design system consistency
- Validate designs through user feedback and testing

### Goals
- Maximize user satisfaction and product usability
- Ensure consistent, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support costs
- Balance user needs with business objectives

### Typical Communication
- Weekly design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and insights
- Design specs and handoff documentation
- Collaboration sessions with product and engineering

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Align on user needs, prioritization, and feature requirements
- **Developers**: Collaborate on implementation details and design feasibility
- **Customer Success/Support**: Gather user feedback and identify pain points

---

## Customer Success/Support

### Role Summary
Customer Success/Support professionals ensure customers achieve their desired outcomes when using the product. They provide assistance, gather feedback, and advocate for customer needs across the organization.

### Responsibilities
- Provide technical support and troubleshooting
- Onboard new customers and ensure product adoption
- Gather and communicate customer feedback to product teams
- Document common issues and create support resources
- Monitor customer health and proactively address concerns
- Escalate critical issues and feature requests

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce time to resolution for customer issues
- Increase product adoption and usage
- Provide actionable customer insights to product teams

### Typical Communication
- Daily ticket triage and customer communications
- Weekly sync with product and engineering on top issues
- Monthly reports on support trends and feature requests
- Escalation notifications for critical customer issues

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Share customer feedback and feature requests to inform roadmap
- **Developers**: Escalate bugs and collaborate on reproducing customer issues
- **UX/UI Designers**: Provide user feedback on usability and pain points

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts transform data into actionable insights that inform product decisions, measure outcomes, and identify opportunities for improvement. They design metrics, build dashboards, and analyze trends to support data-driven decision making.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key product metrics and KPIs
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports
- Analyze user behavior and product usage patterns
- Conduct A/B tests and measure experiment results
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in data
- Collaborate with teams to ensure data quality and instrumentation

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making across teams
- Provide timely, accurate insights on product performance
- Identify opportunities for growth and improvement
- Ensure reliable measurement of success metrics

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metric reviews and insights presentations
- Ad-hoc analysis requests and findings
- Quarterly business reviews and trend analysis
- Collaboration on experiment design and instrumentation

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Define success metrics, analyze feature performance, and inform prioritization
- **Developers**: Collaborate on event instrumentation and data pipeline design
- **Project Managers**: Provide metrics on project outcomes and impact
